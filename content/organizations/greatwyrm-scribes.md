As arguably the most extensive knowledge-gathering organization in Ardeo, the Greatwyrm Scribes encompasses a broad spectrum of individuals dedicated to uncovering the mysteries of the past and present. They gather researchers, writers, magic users, traders, hunters, literally everybody who is interested in the history of this world and creatures that inhabit it. They are funded by governments, kings and emperors, even local nobles, since their work is invaluable. New members are free to join any of their many libraries, or travel the world, seeking the adventure, and writing about the experience. 
There are many widespread libraries with countless resources, and their network is pretty developed, so much that you are able to find some books about some rare creatures of Veil Morass, in the libraries of Westglade. Their biggest library is in the Nohenjo Daro, spanning across five floors and more than a thousand square feets.
