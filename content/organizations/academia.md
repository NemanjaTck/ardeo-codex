Academia, with its roots tracing back to the city of Altamere, has undergone several transformations before adopting its current name. Its founding members were pivotal in establishing Nohenjo Daro, carrying with them a trove of arcane knowledge. This repository of secrets and rituals was always safeguarded, accessible solely to those of high rank within the order.
Neverending search for knowledge, magic artifacts and items, scrolls and rituals, led them to the most distant parts of the world.
Many of the high ranking mages of the Academia were killed during the War of the Elements, as the Academia had all of the Zenari, Elves and Humans as their members. Despite the loss of many distinguished mages, Academia remains resilient, actively seeking new talents from its headquarters in Nohenjo Daro. The Academia Archives in Nohenjo Daro holds a vast collection of magical and arcane lore and artifacts.
