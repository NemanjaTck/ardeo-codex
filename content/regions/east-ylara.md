East Ylara is the region of Eurenis, encircling almost the whole eastern coast of the continent and one part of the north one. It is the second biggest region of the continent.

### Wildlife

Temperate climate suits most of the life forms, and therefore East Ylara is abundant with different animal and plant species.
- Nehkat is a rare in the southern parts around the Free Cities, and is mostly seen in the northern parts, in the forest around [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/) and far to the northern elven outposts on the [North Stjarna sea](/codex/nature/north-stjarna-sea/)
- Two-headed snake is a specie of snakes, that is highly present in this region
- Rumors say that northern forests are home to Xaszar but it is not confirmed
- Usual and less rare animal species are found in forest and plains - wolves, bears, deer. wild horses etc.

### Plants

- Blood Tear is found only in the [Dusk Forest](/codex/nature/dusk-forest/) to the north, and some spots in the [Petrified Forest](/codex/nature/petrified-forest/)

### Climate and geography

- Climate is pretty temperate, throughout the whole year. Summer and winters are mild.
- Hurricanes are a rare sight, but they happen. Two hundreds years ago, Planum has built a small outpost on the coast of the [Sea of Alundir](/codex/nature/sea-of-alundir/), that was completely destroyed during one of the hurricanes.
- [Hash'at](/codex/culture/hashat/) - During spring and autumn, strong winds sometimes push the clouds from [Heg Dragal](/codex/regions/heg-dragal/) into the surrounding regions. Therefore southern and south-western areas of East Ylara could be hit by the acid rain.
- East Ylara is mostly plain region with no mountain ranges
- [Lake Eletheri](/codex/nature/lake-eletheri/) and the third biggest river on the continent [River Eltheri](/codex/nature/river-eltheri/) are the biggest water bodies

### Natural resources

- Salt - Cities of [Icaria](/codex/settlements/icaria/) and [Planum](/codex/settlements/planum/) are famous for its salt refineries and special line of boats "Salt hunters", specialized for salt gathering.
- Silver - Village of [Casta](/codex/villages/casta/) is built on top of a silver mine in [Eltheri Peaks](/codex/nature/eltheri-peaks/)
- Farms - Land south of [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/) and between [Icaria](/codex/settlements/icaria/) and [Vuk](/codex/settlements/vuk/) is highly fertile. Many villages in the surrounding area are living of farming crops, wheat and hops production, beer and wine making.
- Stone - There is a big quarry south of [Vuk](/codex/settlements/vuk/) on the road to Tardum that belongs to the Free Cities. There is one more in the north, in the hills east of [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/), under the control of Sun Elves. There are more smaller ones, but not important for mention.
- Diamonds - Next to the quarry east of [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/), there is a small diamond mine, highly protected day and night.
- Fish and seafood - Ports of [Icaria](/codex/settlements/icaria/) and [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/) are both plentiful with fish. You can always find fresh fish and seafood in the taverns and inns around the city.

### People and cultures

Region of East Ylara spreads through the lands of the [Sun Elves](/codex/races/sun-elves/) in the north and the Free Cities to the south. [Galadhal](/codex/settlements/galadhal/) is a city with mostly elven population, while Free cities are a complete mix of different races and cultures.
