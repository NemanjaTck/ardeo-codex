After the War of the Elements, process of closing the gates and the war itself left such a devastation in these areas. First gate was opened in Ekresh Desolace, second in the south of Veil Morass, third in the Heg Dragal, and fourth and the last in the mines near Ulfheim. Due to the regions destruction, ash and waste is brought up in the air, forming fiery black and red clouds. In the regions of gates, this fire rain, called Hash'at is falling during the whole year round. During spring and autumn, strong winds sometimes push the clouds into the surrounding regions. It is believed that those rains are brought by the goddess of fire Inis, due the her imprisonment, and that she cursed the world.
When the rain starts, creatures should evade being outside. Every drop is causing acid and fire damage. Cities have developed different protection methods, however it is never advised to stay outside during the Hash'at.
From the moment of gate closing, forces of old alliance Amalgam, are guarding the gates, day by date. Gates are surrounded by watchtowers and guards are ever present. The only unguarded gate is the one in the north, lost in the mines, with what is believed without a single person knowing its exact location.
