The far western major cities on the Westglade continent have come together to form the powerful Qi-Yian Trade League. The league's founding members were Mendalis (now known as Segmut) and Yuzunu. After discovering the Shalari Elves, the chancellors from Segmut and Yuzunu saw potential allies in them, leading to the inclusion of Daksun as the final member of the League. The primary aim of this union is the economic wellbeing and growth of its members. While the signed alliance does not include military support, Yuzunu, initially serving as a shipyard for Mendalis, has provided defense support whenever Mendalis faced threats. In recent years, the Trade League has been vocal about expansion, culminating in settlers traversing the ocean to establish a presence on the eastern side of the Veli Morass continent.

##### Life

Members of the Qi-Yian Trade League are skilled sailors with a strong and versatile fleet. Life in Segmut and Yuzunu contrasts with that of the Shalari Elves. The humans of Yuzunu are world-renowned shipbuilders, taking pride in their woodworking and wood carving skills. Segmut, with its multicultural environment, prides itself on trade and profit. In stark contrast, the Shalari lead peaceful lives. The diverse cultures within the Trade League contribute to a rich tapestry of different gods, holidays, celebrations, and events, all of which the Qi-Yian Trade League recognizes and embraces.

##### Diplomacy

The Qi-Yian Trade League has established trade agreements with Ragdar, Moravia, the Commonwealth of Clover, and many other entities. The League typically abstains from involvement in wars unless there is imminent danger and a collective decision is made through voting. Segmut hosts embassies for most of the factions within the city, facilitating diplomatic engagements and international relations.
