As the first settlers in the north dedicated themselves to peace, rebuilding, and growth, some harbored thoughts solely of revenge against the Ragdar Kingdom. A faction of the exiles, led by Dogur, crossed the mountains to settle in a remote northern bay of Westglade. Utilizing wood from nearby forests, they quickly built a formidable fleet. Naming themselves Clan Bjornblod, they devoted their lives to pillaging the Ragdar coasts, vowing to continue until they could reclaim their homeland. This deep-seated vendetta eventually spiraled into madness, leading some rogue Bjornblod ships to raid the coasts of Hvitil and Moravia. The Bjornblod fleet suffered a significant setback when a combined Moravian and Hammarborg force destroyed half of their ships.

At the onset of the War of the Elements, mired in conflicts with most neighboring factions, Clan Bjornblod chose not to support any side in the war. However, there are tales of some captains who took their ships to fight alongside the Zenari, seizing the chance to confront Ragdar and Moravia.

##### Life

Clan Bjornblod is primarily based in the city of Herferborg, with only a few other settlements along the icy coasts. Known for its corruption and criminal activity, Herferborg lacks any significant traditions. Dwarves of the Bjornblod clan follow their Captains and Chiefs with little respect for other Dwarven Clans or Human states. Their main source of income is raiding, with successful raiders often promoted to Dwarven Captains, commanding their own fleets. Women generally do not participate in raids; instead, they stay home to care for children and engage in various support professions that sustain the city.

##### Diplomacy

The ancient adversary of Clan Bjornblod is the Ragdar Empire. The dwarves of Bjornblod have not forgotten their exile and the denial of their right to return to their homeland. While there is no active war between these factions, relations remain tense. Clan Bjornblod is hostile towards all major factions and organizations and has historically eschewed alliances. The only cooperation they have participated in involves joint defense initiatives and expeditions to the north, battling creatures and beasts alongside the Eldrar clan.

##### Important characters

- Ergumd "The Thirsty" Wolfblood
    Manipulative and mischievous chieftain of the clan. Without any honor and dignity, he would be ready to put his entire clan at stake, if there is a chance for a massive treasure.

- Lokhart Wolfblood, dwarven cleric and son of Ergumd
    He currently leads High Council, trying to steer the decisions his father makes, to ensure that his clan has any future at all.

- Hannah "The Red Fury", famous Dwarven Captain
    One of the greatest captains Bjornblod clan ever had. Even though Dwarven Captains are all equal in rank, she commands biggest fleet, and is feared by most of the other captains.
