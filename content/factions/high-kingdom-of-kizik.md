The K'izik, the oldest of the Zenari tribes, established a temple dedicated to the gods and their priests, which rapidly developed into the largest city on the Westglade continent. Known for its magnificent architecture and power, K'izik swiftly ascended to the status of a Kingdom, governed by a High King. The High Kingdom of K'izik is the largest Zenari faction, stretching from the capital city of Maanar'Kin in the north to the desolate wastes of Ekresh in the south. The tribe of Hel, nestled in the northwestern mountains, is also part of Kizik.

##### Life

In the Kingdom of K'izik, there is a saying: "nobody is weak." This ethos is embodied by a custom observed on the last day of each month when everyone donates what they can to assist those in need. On this day, taverns provide meals at no cost to the homeless and poor, leatherworkers donate clothing, and traders give extra silvers or other goods. The cities within the Kingdom are large and diverse in their dominant professions. While most weapons, metal, and stone are imported from other Zenari factions, K'izik exports food to the cities of Chandar and Dogbor, maintaining strong relations with other Zenari tribes.

##### Diplomacy

The Kingdom of K'izik is actively working to improve relations primarily with Elven states. These relations remain tense, largely because K'izik refuses to cede any territory or pay war reparations. However, in a gesture of goodwill, they have dispatched their workforce and architects to assist with rebuilding efforts in the affected regions.

##### Important characters

- Xasundah, High Queen
    With Kan'Monah leaving no successor, highest ranked priestess took over the command of the Storm Zenari. She is harsh, but just and disciplined.

- Tezuo, High Priest of the Hel tribe
    Successor of Shahtani, and one of the Arcana Zenari. Tall, beautiful, but mute, due to the curse of his people.

- Loxogur "The Hawk", captain of the Xudon
    Loxogur, funny, charming, relatively young Zenari, is experienced captain of the sky ships, and is currently captain of the Zenari fleet flagship - Xudon
