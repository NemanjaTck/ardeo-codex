In the verdant forest lands of Moravia, two Human states, the Knyazhevina of Veles and the Knyazhevina of Volod, together form the Tsardom of Moravia. The Humans of Moravia settled in this region with respect for its previous occupants—spirits and creatures from the forests and lakes. Now, they coexist in peaceful harmony, respecting each other and united in defense of the lush Moravian lands. The cities of Veles and Volod serve as the capitals and largest cities of their respective states. The region is safeguarded by the agile and vigilant Greenblade Battalion, with elite Stag Knights patrolling the outskirts and distant borders of the Tsardom.

##### Life

The people of Moravia revere the guidance of nature spirits, respecting the forest's beasts and creatures and learning from them. Many cities and villages adopt different animals and mythical beasts as symbols of protection, which are displayed on their flags and banners and even influence the names of places. Apart from Veles and Volod, towns and villages in Moravia typically lack fortifications; some are enclosed by wooden fences, while others remain open. Their intimate knowledge of the terrain and adept scouting provide a strategic advantage over any intruders.

Common professions include woodcutters, hunters, and fishermen, reflecting the community's deep connection with their natural environment.


##### Diplomacy

Moravia was the first to respond to the call of the Elves, sending entire regiments of the Greenblade Battalion. There exists an ancient alliance and a deep-seated respect between the Humans of Moravia and the Elves. While relations with other Human factions remain neutral, Moravia and Ragdar have historically collaborated, notably in the Battle of Hazimat Fields and the Siege of Solarion. Moravia maintains friendly relations with the Hammarborg clan, yet its interactions with the other two clans on the eastern side of Westglade are tense and often hostile.
