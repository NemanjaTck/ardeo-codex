Clan Eldrar originated as one of the clans within Bjornblod, under the leadership of Chief Dogur Bjornblod. Initially, their people served as scouts, venturing west towards the mountains and mines. Eventually, they settled in this new region, where their affinity for fire and metal allowed them to advance their metallurgy and blacksmithing skills to exceptional levels. Over time, valuing the mines over the sea, Clan Eldrar transitioned from a protectorate and vassal of Bjornblod to an independent clan. Despite gaining independence, their shared animosity towards Ragdar persisted, leading them to participate in joint land and sea actions and raids with Bjornblod. However, being isolated in the mountains and lacking a powerful fleet, Clan Eldrar gradually grew distant from Bjornblod, leading to strained relations.

##### Life

Living a secluded life within the secure confines of the Bargahai mountains, the people of Eldrar seldom leave their main city, Eldrekar. While there are a few minor settlements nearby, Eldrekar remains the hub of activity for the clan. The society comprises miners, blacksmiths, artisans, masons, and writers, all united in their goal towards the future prosperity of the Eldrar people. They worship the goddess of fire, Inis, and Koval, the protector deity of blacksmiths. An annual event known as the **Days of Fire** is a significant cultural practice where animals are sacrificed in fire pits to receive blessings from the gods.

##### Diplomacy

Apart from their historical ties with Bjornblod, Clan Eldrar has minimal diplomatic interactions with other factions. Their city, nestled within the mountains and accessible only through gates, maintains a fortified posture with only a few settlements outside of it. Entry into the city requires approval from their forces stationed in these outlying settlements, reflecting their insular and guarded nature.

##### Important characters

- Eurin Eldrar
    Old, wise and too cautious are the words that describe Eurin. Eldrar clan increased their defenses massively during his mandate as a chief.

- Godor Eldrar
    He currently leads High Council, and is a younger brother of Eurin. More brave than his brother, however, less brains.

- Thyra Dagmar
    Lead architect of the city projects. She knows every single tunnel and dungeon of Eldrekar.
