The first exiles from the Ragdar Kingdom, led by Esteban and Illric, arrived at a mysterious lake on the western side of the Bargahai mountains. After witnessing the first storms and thunder striking the lake, they named their settlement Eye of Tahrid and the lake Tear of Tahrid, respectively. Stripped of his former name, Illric renamed himself and his followers Stormson, founding what would later become the strongest Dwarven clan. They never engaged in full-scale war again, although there were smaller factions within the clan that periodically raided the border villages and cities of the Ragdar Kingdom. Ragdar launched several offensives into Dwarven lands, all of which were unsuccessful due to the harsh winter and rugged terrain of the Bargahai mountains.

##### Life

Sunshine is a rare occurrence in the northern frozen lands. The harsh conditions of the Bargahai mountains limit resources, thus diminishing the need for some professions while necessitating others. The most popular professions are fishermen and hunters, while sailors are highly respected throughout the lands of the Stormson clan. Dwarves join ship crews at a very young age and by the time they mature, they are already seasoned sailors.

The founding of the city of Eye of Tahrid is celebrated across every Stormson settlement on the 27th of Ineos, marking the day slavery ended and the exiles found their new home. A period of fierce thunderstorms that lasts for approximately two weeks each year hits the lake during this time. During these storms, the dwarves of the Stormson clan typically work less and spend more time celebrating and drinking, honoring Tahrid, the god of storms.


##### Diplomacy

The ancient enemy of the Stormson clan is the Ragdar Empire. The dwarves will never forget that as exiles, they were never allowed to return to their homeland. Currently, there is no ongoing war between these two factions, but their relations remain strained. During the war, the Stormson clan forged new alliances with Elven factions, the Tsardom of Moravia, and even established a new trade route to the distant continent of Veil Morass, engaging with Gnomes and Halflings residing there. The Stormson clan maintains a close relationship with Clan Hammarborg but remains completely neutral towards the other two clans.

##### Important characters

- Svaldor Stormson, current chief of Stormson clan
    Proud and righteous, brother of Borin Stormson, took helm after Borin left for the war and never returned. 

- Astrid Odror, dwarven cleric
    Famous adventurer, in charge of Dwarven outposts on the island of Noerdgard. She is leading expeditions into the further north on the island trying to resolve all mysteries around it.

- Ramagun Odir, famous dwarven druid and alchemist
    One of the greatest alchemists in the north. Legends tell that there is not a disease Ramagun can not handle with his potions and salves.

- Ase Ivarrson, Voice of the North
    Bard and actor, one of the most beautiful dwarves that ever lived, she is known to perform around the world, however always likes to spend winters home
