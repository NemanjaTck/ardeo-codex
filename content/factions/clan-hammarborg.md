The first settlers of the Hvitil island established the city of Eirdar and laid the foundations of the Hammarborg clan. They ran away from the war torn lands in the north, looking for a safe haven. Recognizing the bay's strategic and aesthetic significance, they quickly constructed a majestic shipyard, rivaling even the famed shipyard of Yuzunu in grandeur and craftsmanship. The ships of Hammarborg, renowned for their resilience and ability to navigate through the harshest of storms, soon became a symbol of the clan's prowess and determination. The docks of Viktor Bay evolved into a bustling trade center, connecting the northern hemisphere of Ardeo.

##### Life

The mines in the Magadur mountains are the primary source of production for the Hammarborg clan. Recognizing the wealth beneath the mountains, they have constructed extensive tunnels that lead directly from the mines to their largest city, Eirdar. This city's docks are a hive of activity, bustling with traders and ships that transport ore and refined metals to other continents. This infrastructure not only supports the clan's economy but also enhances its strategic importance in regional trade.

##### Diplomacy

The Hammarborg clan was responsive to the call of the Elves during the war, sending their fleet to fight alongside Borin Stormson. Represented by Enge Fireborn, the clan's leader, Hammarborg signed the Amalgam agreement, aligning themselves with a powerful alliance that would eventually defend Solarion and contribute significantly to winning the war. While they maintain hostile relations with the Bjornblod clan, Hammarborg otherwise portrays itself as a peaceful and just clan, open to welcoming everyone. This openness has helped them foster a reputation as fair and reliable partners in the broader community.

##### Important characters

- Davadar "the Gray" Hammarborg, chief
    Young but educated chief, that gathered best people around him, to evade making mistakes until he gains experience

- Enge Fireborn, legendary admiral
    Led a fleet of Hammarborg clan into the war. She was on the winning side in the battle of Galadhal bay and later on Siege of Solarion, with remarkable achievements as the admiral of the fleet. She returned to Eirdar as a hero.

- Sidard Hammarborg, head of the Davadar personal council
    Uncle of Davadar, wise and old ex-admiral, Sidard, plays an important role in the Hammarbog clan. He is the closest advisor to Davadar.
