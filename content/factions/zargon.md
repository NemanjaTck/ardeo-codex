Humans in this region are the direct descendants of Sun Elf priests and scientists. Zargon, the first Human state, has Nohenjo Daro as its capital, which serves as the cradle of Human civilization and, along with Solarion, a center for magic, research, and innovation. Despite their deep connections with Elves, Zargon's society operates differently, being a meritocracy governed by the educated and powerful.

The government of Zargon is chosen by the people. The state has no active military; instead, cities are heavily fortified and protected by an active city guard. The Great Library in Nohenjo Daro collects writings from historians, wizards, and scientists worldwide. Those keen on expanding their knowledge often seek to join the academies of Nohenjo Daro or Solarion. Zargon is also highly religious, with each city choosing gods as their patrons and often centering around ziggurats as principal temple buildings.

##### Life

Education, research, and arcana are the pillars of life in Zargon. Home to the world's foremost academies and libraries, the state promotes starting education at an early age. Academy professors hold the highest respect and positions within the Zargon government, as the city prioritizes research and science over warfare and combat. The national holiday, the **Day of Victory**, commemorates the decisive victory over the Zenari forces during the War of the Elements. It is celebrated with a grand carnival and theater shows that display the prowess of Zargon mages and warriors. It is customary for families to send their children abroad, often to Elvish cities, to study different cultures and creatures, returning to Zargon enriched with new wisdom and knowledge.


##### Diplomacy

High Magister Ermund Zad the Second continues to maintain the relationships established by his predecessors. As one of the cities that did not suffer occupation during the war, Nohenjo Daro was the site where peace treaties were signed after the gates were closed. Zargon enjoys robust political ties with all factions, maintaining a strict alliance with Solarion and holding research and trade agreements with many others. Zargon's cities have open policies regarding foreigners and organizations, welcoming guilds and global factions warmly.

##### Important characters

- Ermund Zad the Second, High Magister
    High Magister and the current leader of Zargon. Smart, humble and loyal to his people, he always puts the interests of Zargon before his own.

- Dondur Assednazzar, lead professor of Amaranth academy and Great Library keeper
    Specializing in alchemy and celestial bodies, Dondur, older Sun Elf gentleman, is currently the head of Amaranth academy, and lead keeper of the Great Library.

- Azalia Loer, current High Mage of Academia
    Powerful wizard, relatively young and bright Human, current leader of the global faction of Academia.
