Eastern Zenari land is enveloped in lush jungles and traversed by the mighty K’uhad River. The settlements of Mayek begin at the river's source and extend across the jungle, with the city of Aghanat at the eastern edge, which was once on the border with Elven states but now lies on the coast of a newly formed ocean. Before the war, Mayek was a rapidly developing part of the Zenari Empire, where the Water Zenari used their newfound powers to enhance the land and its settlements. Mayek had established trade agreements with the Setareh Federation, facilitating the import of metals and the export of leather, food, and wood.

After the death of Kan’monah, Ner’thazal, the High Priest of Mayek, ascended as the second and last emperor of the Zenari Empire. At the war's end, Aghanat fell to the forces of Elves and Dragonguards, significantly impacting the city's future. Once the Elves withdrew and the continents divided at the war's conclusion, Aghanat found itself on the shores of a newly formed ocean, opening new possibilities for the people of Mayek. With Ner’thazal's death during the war, the new High Priest Ugdur the Short took charge, initiating the reconstruction of Aghanat and its surrounding settlements.

##### Life

Mayek, like other Zenari states, operates as a theocracy where the role of a priest is highly respected and not just anyone can fill it. Priests make all significant decisions, with local governance and laws administered among them according to rank. The High Priest, as the head of state, makes crucial decisions regarding external relations and overall governance.

Due to Aghanat's ongoing reconstruction, all cities and villages actively contribute resources and provide construction workers, architects, blacksmiths, and others, as Ugdur strives to restore Aghanat to its former splendor. Celebrations honoring Avelia are largely prohibited, as the Ocean Zenari believe she led them astray. However, the docks retain her name as a nod to her contributions before the war.


##### Diplomacy

Following the war and Avelia's silence, Ugdur focused on mending ties with former allies. Mayek is the sole Zenari state to have signed a war reparations agreement with the opposing side, specifically with the Setareh Federation. Through war reparations and payments, they have revitalized and enhanced their previous trade agreement to the benefit of both parties. With its network of canals, the city of Aghanat is rapidly becoming comparable to the great trade cities of the world.

##### Important characters

- Ugdur the Short, High Priest of Mayek
    Not the greatest warrior due to his physical capabilities, however, great priest and trader. He is leading the expansion and growth of Mayek.

- Re'razan, city architect
    Always busy and in a rush. Leading a team of architects and builders that intersected the whole city of Aghanat, making it fully traversable by boat. This feat is considered so unique and complex, that many regard it as a wonder.

- Azudu, ex-soldier and guide
    One of the survivors of the war, now being a guide through the jungles of Mayek. She is happy now, as she is returned to the true call.
