Volod, a city founded in 1384 BG, was established five years after the Elves founded Veles. It is nestled on the shores of the crystal-clear waters of Lake Volodia. The first settlers encountered the Vodanoi, water spirits who are guardians of the lake and its inhabitants. Today, the citizens of Volod live in harmony with the lake and the surrounding nature.

#### Population

22,500 (64% Moravia human, 14% elves, 12% other human races, 10% other races)

#### Government

The Tsardom of Moravia, established in 1012 BG by Bogoslav Meronevich, positioned the Meronevich dynasty as rulers for centuries. The current Tsar, Knyaz Iliya Meronevich, resides in Veles, the capital of Knyazhevina Veles. Volod serves as the capital of Knyazhevina Volod, with the current Knyaz being Egor Ranuzin. As a part of the Tsardom of Moravia, Knyaz Egor reports to Tsar Iliya Meronevich.

#### City defenses

Protected by the Greenblade Battalion, a standing army present in both capital cities, Volod's defense includes high-ranking officers, druids, shapeshifters, and over a hundred soldiers within the city. The elite Stag Knights patrol the outskirts, safeguarding the forests and borders.

#### Organizations

The city's significant quarry to the west, being the main source of stone export, has fostered local worker guilds and syndicates. The Circle of Gaia has a presence in Volod, alongside representatives from other global organizations.

#### Architecture

Moravian architecture, honoring a deep respect for nature, seamlessly integrates green spaces, trees, and plants into the cityscape.

**Highmountain Quarry**: Located to the west of the city, this extensive quarry features multiple levels and three excavation sites, with stone being the city's primary source of income through export.

**Elemental Wellspring**: Erected as a tribute to the Vodanoi spirits by Volod's first settlers, the Elemental Wellspring now stands at the city's heart. Legend has it that drinking from its sacred waters bestows clarity and wisdom. The wellspring is vigilantly guarded at all times.

#### Resources

Positioned by a lake and in agreement with the water spirits, the city has prohibited any construction on the lake itself. Small boats are permitted for fishing to sustain the city's population, without overharvesting. The surrounding forests provide wood, berries, and game, while the city's main production and export are stones from the Highmountain Quarry.

#### Lore

Renowned as a peaceful haven, many choose to spend their final days enjoying the serenity of Volod and its surroundings. Although the Greenblade Battalion recruits from the city, Volod has never faced the threat of war or attack. The only notable event in Volod's history was a rebellion against the tyrannical Tsar Agor the Second, led by Knyaginya Natalia Vitarin, the last of her dynasty.
