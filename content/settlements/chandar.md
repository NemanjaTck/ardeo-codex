Chandar, the capital of the Inis Protectorate, is the birthplace of the Flame Zenari and home to the ancient Ixil tribe. Rising from the ashes of its former self, which was destroyed by volcanic fire, Chandar has not only recovered but also mastered and harnessed the very forces that once devastated it.

#### Population

31,700 (70% Flame Zenari, 25% other Zenari, 5% other races)

#### Government

The Inis Protectorate is governed authoritatively by a single leader, known as the Flame Zenari High Priest or the Protector. This title is held for life, and a new Protector is chosen from among the most influential and capable Zenari only after the current one's death.

#### City defenses

Chandar is naturally fortified by rugged and inaccessible terrain, eliminating the need for city walls. All city entrances are secured by stone watchtowers and regular guard patrols. Besides the city guard, members of the Flame Zenari army are always present, with local mages ready to harness and direct the fire from Vudrush volcano against any invaders.

#### Organizations

The constant presence of fire, ash, and heat creates harsh living conditions, deterring many who are not accustomed to such an environment. Consequently, other races and global organizations are rare in Chandar, visiting but seldom staying long.

#### Architecture

Chandar is renowned for its black-colored buildings, constructed using black volcanic rock that is fortified through arcane and chemical processes for both construction and agricultural purposes. The interiors of these buildings are insulated against heat, and special plants are cultivated in dusted black rock.

**Thousand Years Flame**: The city's central square features a majestic brazier with an eternal flame said to have burned for over a thousand years, making it the oldest known eternal fire in the world.

**Volcanic Forges**: A portion of the city is integrated into the volcano itself. The Flame Zenari of Chandar are metallurgical experts, mastering the elements of fire and metal to produce advanced weaponry and structures.

**Mage Quarters**: Mages hold a prestigious position within the Flame Zenari forces. An entire district is dedicated to the mastery of arcana, complete with libraries, academies, and shops.

#### Resources

Chandar excels in metallurgy, with weapons and construction materials serving as its primary exports. Due to a scarcity of natural resources, most other essential supplies for the city's inhabitants are imported.

#### Lore

The lands of Ekresh were once fertile and lush with resources. However, after the Zenari settlement, subsequent droughts and tribal wars over resources ravaged the land, leading to its current desolation, now known as Ekresh Desolace. Chandar was originally founded adjacent to a mountain, later identified as the Vuldrush volcano. Its first eruption devastated the city with lava and ash, claiming nearly half of its population. The survivors rebuilt Chandar, learning to control the volcano's power and use it to their advantage. Following the unification of the tribes into the Inis Protectorate, Chandar became the capital of the newly formed Flame Zenari, serving as the formation site and launching point for their northern military campaigns.
