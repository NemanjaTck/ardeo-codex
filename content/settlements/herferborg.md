The people of Bjornblod harbor a deep-seated disdain for laws, driven by their quest for vengeance against the Ragdar Kingdom—the very people who expelled them from their ancestral lands. Herferborg, situated at the northernmost tip of the Westglade continent, serves as a welcoming port for all, from exiles and prisoners to traders and warriors.

#### Population

15000 (60% Dwarves, 15% Humans, 5% Gnomes, 20% other races)

#### Government

Herferborg is the largest settlement of the Bjornblod clan. In line with Dwarven tradition, the city does not have a ruler but is instead overseen by a caretaker in the role of a chieftain. Each family in the city elects a representative to partake in the political structure. The city governance includes a People's Council for daily and local matters and a High Council for significant issues such as foreign relations and defense. The current chieftain is Ergumd "The Thirsty" Wolfblood.

#### City defenses

The Bjornblod clan boasts a formidable fleet primarily used for raiding. Their military is organized into smaller units, each commanding up to five ships led by a Dwarf Captain. Several of these units are always stationed within the city.

#### Organizations

Herferborg, a port city with lax laws, attracts various thieves' guilds and smaller criminal organizations. The Raven Syndicate, the Tajir Guild, and the Golden Hand all have strong presences here.

#### Architecture

The city's architecture predominantly features wood and stone, with most buildings constructed for functionality over aesthetics.
**Chief Lodge**: The standout structure in Herferborg, resembling a gigantic ship flipped upside down, serves as the living quarters for most of the Bjornblods.
**City Breweries**: Renowned for producing the world's finest mead, Herferborg is home to four distinct breweries, each claiming superiority. The Bjornblod clan annually hosts a festival where brewers compete for the prestigious title of the year's best mead.
**City Docks**: Known for its low-quality taverns, brothels, and subpar food and drink, the city docks cater to those looking to skirt the law and seek entertainment.

#### Resources

The Bjornblod clan and the city of Herferborg primarily amass their resources through raiding, targeting smaller trading ships and coastal villages. Their reluctance to pursue larger fleets into the northern icy waters has shaped their strategy. Additionally, they maintain a fishing fleet and wharfs for food provision.

#### Lore

Founded soon after the Tear of Tahrid, the city of Herferborg and its inhabitants have never sought a peaceful existence. Instead, they yearn to reclaim their homeland and dismantle the Ragdar Kingdom. However, the lack of a substantial military force and the inland location of Ragdar cities have thwarted any significant assaults. The Bjornblod clan also maintains hostile relations with neighboring communities, including the mountain city of Eldrekar.
