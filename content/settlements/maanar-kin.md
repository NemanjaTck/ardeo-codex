Maanar-Kin is the first city founded by the Zenari tribes and is currently the most populous city on the Westglade continent. It has played a significant role in the development of Zenari society throughout history and served as the capital of the ancient Zenari Empire. Today, it remains the home of the Storm Zenari and the High King Priest of the High Kingdom of Kizik.

#### Population

80,000 (50% Storm Zenari, 25% other Zenari, 10% Humans, 5% Elves, 10% other races)

#### Government

The High Kingdom of Kizik is governed by the High King, a title evolved from High Priest. The High King, with the assistance of a personally selected advisory board, makes all major decisions for the kingdom's welfare. Each advisor specializes in a specific area of governance.

#### City defenses

The standing Storm Zenari army, Ard K'uh or "divine warriors" in Zenari, is present in both the capital and every Storm Zenari settlement. Regular regiments and warriors in training typically handle daily patrols and city safeguarding, while the main army regiments address more significant threats.

#### Organizations

As a vast city, Maanar-Kin hosts embassies and seats of most global factions and organizations, alongside popular local guilds and smaller entities.

#### Architecture

"Grandeur" best captures the majestic architecture of Maanar-Kin, where masonry and construction rank among the most advanced professions.

**Kan Mon'ah Skyport**: Named after the first Zenari emperor and High Priest, Kan Mon'ah, this massive floating skyport doubles as a marketplace, accessible only by air. The reasons behind its defiance of gravity remain a mystery and are largely unexplored.

**Nature Reserve**: The city features a wildlife sanctuary closed to visitors, where Zenari care for injured or lost animals before releasing them back into the wild.

**Temple Crypts**: Located east of the city, in the hills, are small temples where Storm Zenari believe that the deceased's soul will retain all buried possessions in the Soul realm. Leaders are buried with their riches, and sometimes their wives are buried alive to join them in the afterlife.

**Temple of the Emperor**: Standing in the north of the city, adjacent to the mountain, the Temple of the Emperor is the city's tallest structure, housing the current High King and his family.

#### Resources

The city's geographical diversity, with fertile fields to the south, mountains to the northwest, and a jungle to the north, provides a wealth of resources. The jungle yields exotic foods and animals, while mills along the K'uhad River process wheat from the southern fields. Cotton and linen are common materials for the city's clothing.

#### Lore

Since its founding, Maanar-Kin has been the zenith of Zenari civilization, enduring through rulers, architectural evolutions, and devastations only to emerge stronger each time. It became the Zenari Empire's capital before the War of the Elements. Following Ner'thazal's death and Aghanat's fall, the remaining Zenari forces retreated to Maanar-Kin's formidable walls to defend against the Amalgam forces. It was here that Zenari and Amalgam factions devised their plan to assault the Demon Gates.
