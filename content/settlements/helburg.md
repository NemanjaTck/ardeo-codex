A strong, fortified keep city located in the Bargahai mountains, Helburg was initially established to support nearby mines before growing into one of the largest cities in the north.

#### Population

20,000 (65% Ragdar Human, 15% Dwarves, 12% other Humans, 8% other races)

#### Government

The Ragdar Kingdom is governed by a diarchy, with two kings ruling jointly. Helburg serves as the seat for one of the Ragdar Kingdom's kings and also hosts an appointed High Marshal, a former military officer, who acts as the governor.

#### City defenses

An active contingent of the Ragdar army is permanently stationed in the city. Additionally, the City Guard, primarily focused on minor issues and enforcing magic prohibition, maintains order within the city.

#### Organizations

The local faction of the Cloaks acts as law enforcers and advisors to the king. Helburg also houses branches of the Tajir Guild and Leari Order, alongside a smaller presence of other factions. Mining and blacksmithing guilds hold significant influence in the city.

#### Architecture

Constructed on the slopes of the Bargahai mountain, Helburg utilizes the mountain itself as natural fortification. While Ulfheim may be larger and more aesthetically pleasing, serving as the diplomatic hub of the kingdom, Helburg is crucial for its resource provision. Consequently, substantial investments have been made in its fortifications.

**City Gates**: Enormous stone gates mark the city's entrance, standing about 50 feet tall and reinforced with thick stone and metal bars. They are adorned with beautifully carved symbols of the two kings, representing both Ulfheim and Helburg, and are always manned by at least 10 guards.

**Rune Vault**: A vault within the city hall treasury secures the riches of both kings. It features iron-reinforced doors and is engraved with runes imbued with a special type of magic, accessible only to the kings' designated vault keepers.

**Mountain Taverns**: Along the mountain slopes and paths leading to the city on the eastern side, numerous taverns offer a selection of meats and drinks, boasting magnificent views of Helburg from above.

#### Resources

The multitude of mines surrounding Helburg are the principal resource providers for the entire Ragdar Kingdom. While Helburg primarily exports resources, it relies on Ulfheim for food supplies delivered by guarded caravans and utilizes small mountain springs for water.

#### Lore

Originally founded as a mining hub, Helburg evolved into a fortified city. Under Lamga rule, the Cloaks exploited slaves for relentless mining. A monument and graveyard honor those who perished during this era. The liberation of the city by Illric, with assistance from Esteban, led to the abolition of slavery and an exodus of many inhabitants. The appointment of Jotar as king by his brother Eormen, recognizing the dangers of magic, led to a strict but fair governance over both cities, with a ban on magic and a revitalization of the Cloaks.

Today, Helburg stands as the northern guardian, supplying surrounding keeps on the border with the Dwarves with both manpower and resources.
