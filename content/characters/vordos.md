Gnome, 84, male

**Appearance** -  This gnome scholar has thinning grey hair and wears round spectacles perched on the bridge of his nose. He dresses in a slightly worn but clean scholar's robe, often seen with ink stains on his fingers and sleeves.

**Alignment** - Lawful Neutral

**Occupation** - Scroll Seller/Trader

**Motivation** - Driven by his love for knowledge and ancient texts, he aims to collect and preserve rare scrolls from all corners of the world. His goal is to make these texts available to the public, believing that knowledge should be accessible to everyone.

**Flaws** - He can be overly meticulous and sometimes spends days on details that others find trivial, causing delays in his work.

**Secret** - Among his collection, he secretly possesses a set of scrolls that are said to contain the lost magic of the world, which he fears could be dangerous if found.

**Memorable trait** - He has an habit of reading aloud to himself in various voices, which he believes helps him better understand the texts.
