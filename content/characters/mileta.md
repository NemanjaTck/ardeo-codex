Wood Elf, 38, male

**Appearance** - Bald, with a long brown beard, appearing much older than his actual age. He is always dressed in rags, stained with alcohol and food. He invariably carries a bottle or barrel containing beer.

**Alignment** - Neutral Good

**Occupation** - Brewer

**Motivation** - Mileta is a modest beer vendor who typically collaborates with taverns in the city district where he lives. Although his beer is rumored to be of high quality, he has never sought to expand his business. Once he earns enough money in one city, he packs up his brewing kit and moves to the next.

**Flaws** - Mileta struggles with alcoholism, which significantly affects his sales and profits from brewing. It is said that he consumes half of his produce, leaving only the other half for sale.

**Secret** - In his youth, before he took up brewing, Mileta underwent monk training and was among the top students. Despite his size and weight, he is remarkably fast and agile. There are tales in the cities he has visited of him taking on five or more men in a fight and emerging victorious.

**Memorable Trait** - He can fall asleep mid-sentence and tends to interrupt his speech with burps.
