Halfing, 29, male

**Appearance** -  Despite his small stature, he is very muscular with wild, tangled red hair and numerous battle scars. He dresses in a mash of animal hides and bones, giving him a fearsome look.

**Alignment** - Chaotic Neutral

**Occupation** - Barbarian

**Motivation** - Driven by an insatiable thirst for adventure and battle, he seeks to prove that powerful warrior can come in any size. His goal is to become a legendary warrior whose name is known across all lands.

**Flaws** - Quick to anger and often acts before thinking, leading him into trouble more often than not.

**Secret** - He was a farmer before his warrior life. Disease took his family, but he still blames it on gods, and is looking for a revenge.

**Memorable trait** - Despite his fierce looks, he has a soft spot for animals and can often be seen caring for wounded creatures.
