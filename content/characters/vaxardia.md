Orc, 45, female

**Appearance** -  Tall and strong, she has a muscular build with redish skin and a stern expression. Her hair is shaved on the sides with a braided mohawk on top. She wears the standard armor of the city watch, kept in impeccable condition.

**Alignment** - Neutral

**Occupation** - City Watch/Officier

**Motivation** - Not too many things excite her as leading the troops to war. She is dreaming of becoming a general one day, having her own soldiers.

**Flaws** - With her repulsiveness to the law, she is struggling with morally gray areas.

**Secret** - She secretly supports a network of informants who help her root out corruption within the city, including within her own ranks.

**Memorable trait** - Known for her booming voice, which she uses in commanding her own recruits and maintaining order during crises.
