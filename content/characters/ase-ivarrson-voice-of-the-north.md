Dwarf, 33, female

**Appearance** -  Long brown curly hair, strong brown eyebrows, and a radiant smile characterize her. She pays great attention to her attire, ranging from simple traveler outfits to the splendid dresses she dons for her performances.

**Alignment** - Neutral Good

**Occupation** - Performer/Singer

**Motivation** - Her life's ambition is to perform and sing in every tavern in the world. Known as a renowned traveler and adventurer, she possesses a beautiful voice that has earned her the title "Voice of the North." Her popularity soars, especially in the northern dwarven territories. When she performs in the city's main square, at least half the population gathers to enjoy her talent.

**Flaws** - Pride often obstructs her path to happiness.

**Secret** - Legends hint that the secret behind her enchanting voice is an artifact she always carries with her.

**Memorable trait** - She stands a bit taller and is less broad than the typical dwarven woman. Occasionally, she sings parts of her sentences while speaking.
