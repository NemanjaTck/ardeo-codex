Elf, 147, female

**Appearance** -  Flowing silver hair and bright yellow eyes. She is always in elegant but practical elven clothes, mostly with earth tones.

**Alignment** - Chaotic Good

**Occupation** - Bartender/Herbalist

**Motivation** - To bridge the gap between the natural world and creatures living in it, using her knowledge of herbal remedies and potions to help those in need. She aims to turn her bar into a sanctuary of healing and relaxation.

**Flaws** - Tends to be overly trusting, often putting her at a disadvantage in business dealings.

**Secret** - She is a guardian of an ancient elven herb garden, which is the source of her rare ingredients.

**Memorable trait** - She can communicate with plants, and her bar is always filled with strange, beautiful flowers that seem to move on their own.
