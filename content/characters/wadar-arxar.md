Ocean Zenari, 185, male

**Appearance** - Elderly and dignified, with deep-set of red eyes. His blue skin is wrinkled, describing his age and wisdom. He dresses in long, blue and gray, flowing robes with symbols of his deity.

**Alignment** - Neutral Good

**Occupation** - Priest/Healer

**Motivation** - He seeks to spread the teachings of peace and forgiving, believing that true strength comes from unity and understanding. He is dedicated to healing both physical and spiritual wounds.

**Flaws** - His idealism sometimes blinds him to the harsh realities of the world.

**Secret** - He holds an ancient relic that is said to have miraculous healing powers, but its use comes with a cost.

**Memorable trait** - He always carries a staff that is taller than he is, which glows softly in the presence of evil.
