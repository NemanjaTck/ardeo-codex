**City:** Zenum
Human, 38, male

**Appearance** -  He has slicked-back, black hair and a meticulously groomed beard. His piercing blue eyes contrast with his tanned, weathered skin. Always dressed in a celan white apron over dark trousers and a vest, he exudes an air of professionalism.

**Alignment** - Lawful Neutral

**Occupation** - Bartender/Innkeeper

**Motivation** - To own the most respected tavern in the land, famous not just for its ale but also as a haven for weary travelers and a meeting spot for intriguing personalities. He loves sharing tales of adventurers who've passed through his doors.

**Flaws** - Overly critical of himself and others, especially when it comes to the quality of service and beverages.

**Secret** - He possesses an ancient recipe book for mystical brews that can induce visions or heal ailments, passed down from his mysterious mentor.

**Memorable trait** - Known for his dry humor and the impressive ability to remember every customer's favorite drink.
