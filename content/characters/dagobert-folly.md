Halfling, 45, male

**Appearance** -  Short, messy yet clean gray hair, brown eyes that shine with mirth, and a broad, though insincere, smile. His voice is surprisingly deep for a halfling. He wears a gray leather coat, weathered and sun-faded, paired with brown leather boots showing similar signs of wear.

**Alignment** - Chaotic Neutral

**Occupation** - Traveling merchant.

**Motivation** - He journeys in a cart pulled by two horses, Molly and Beck. Dagobert is driven by the pursuit of wealth, aiming to maximize his gold through trade, acquisition, and resale of valuable items. He tends to overvalue the merchandise he sells and is willing to face perilous quests for lucrative treasures.

**Flaws** - His ineptitude in combat has resulted in the loss of his caravan's goods on three occasions. As a result, he now almost always hires mercenaries for protection during his travels.

**Secret** - Despite his nomadic lifestyle, he secretly loathes traveling and yearns for a settled life with a spouse. However, his inability to communicate with women keeps this dream just out of reach.

**Memorable trait** - One leg is shorter than the other, which contributes to his poor fighting skills and perhaps symbolizes his uneven journey through life.
