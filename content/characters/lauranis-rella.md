lau
Elf, 77, female

**Appearance** -  This elf is graceful with a pale skin color contrasted by her long, jet-black hair. She dresses elegantly in traditional elven robes that are both functional and beautiful, designed for the quiet corridors of her library.

**Alignment** - Neutral Good

**Occupation** - Librarian/Archivist

**Motivation** - She sees herself as a guardian of history and culture, dedicated to preserving and spreading the wisdom contained within her vast library. Her ultimate goal is to guide others toward enlightenment through knowledge.

**Flaws** - She is overly protective of her library's collection, sometimes to the point of denying access to those she deems unworthy or careless.

**Secret** - She has discovered a way to enter and interact with the worlds described in some of the magical books she guards.

**Memorable trait** - Known for her ability to recall any book or scroll's exact location and contents in her library, earning her the nickname "The Living Index" among scholars.
