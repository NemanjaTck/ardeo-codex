Dwarf, 45, male

**Appearance** -  Compact and muscular, he has wild, tangled black hair and a thick beard interwoven with small forest tokens like feathers and beads. His clothes are rugged and functional. He has a leather armor, daggers and a hunting shortbow, designed for life in the wilderness.

**Alignment** - Neutral

**Occupation** - Guide/Hunter

**Motivation** - With a profound connection to the forest, he aims to protect its secrets and beauty from those who seek to exploit it. He guides only those he trusts through the deepest parts of the woods. He never asks for money, but he expects a payment, in trinkets or magic items.

**Flaws** - He is gruff and not very social, making him difficult to approach or befriend.

**Secret** - He knows the location of an ancient grove that is said to be the place of a powerful nature spirit.

**Memorable trait** - Always accompanied by a tame wolf that he raised from a pup, serving both as a companion and a protector.
