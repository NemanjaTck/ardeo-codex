Zenari, 85, female

**Appearance** -  She has a lean, athletic build with weathered skin and a series of tribal tattoos that tell her life story. Her hair is long and braided, with feathers and beads throughout. She wears practical clothing suitable for the jungle environment.

**Alignment** - Neutral

**Occupation** - Guide/Explorer

**Motivation** - With a deep and profound respect for the jungle and its secrets, she is determined to protect its sanctity from those who would harm it. She guides adventurers and researchers to promote understanding and appreciation of the natural world.

**Flaws** - Extremely superstitious, she often interprets natural events as omens, which can alter her decisions unpredictably.

**Secret** - She knows the pathways to a hidden city said to be filled with untold treasures but also cursed.

**Memorable trait** - Her incredible ability to mimic any animal sound has saved her from countless dangers and makes her an invaluable guide in the dense jungle.
