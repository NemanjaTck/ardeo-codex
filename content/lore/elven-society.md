As the Zenari found purpose in their connection with the natural world, the Elves embarked upon a different path, shaping their society around the pillars of research and craftsmanship. They swiftly invented their system of currency and established trading routes that spread through their lands, connecting every major settlement. They were proficient with architecture and masonry. Attacks from wildlife, bandits and remnants of Zenari tribes that settled in the lands of Eurenis, were rare but devastating. In response, Elves fortified every bigger settlement. The first elements of defence took the form of wooden walls, and afterwards with gaining more and more proficiency in masonry, stone walls were built in every important Elven settlement.
Those settlements soon expanded into huge cities, with three biggest ones - Altamere in the central mountains, Galadhal - northern port city, and Nezereth in the western plains.
