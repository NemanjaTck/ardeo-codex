In an effort to quickly erase the memory of the past and the loss of two siblings, the gods turned their focus to Ardeo. They ventured across the land, shaping the continents, breathing life into various creatures, and forming rivers, mountains, and oceans.

At the very end, to ensure harmony in the world, Ishhalor, provided the gift of sentience to four different races, each assigned a unique role in the world of Ardeo.
- Strong and resilient Zenari, became guardians of the natural order.
- Beautiful and charismatic Elves, were artists and guardians of culture.
- Agile and skilful Raod'gar, dwellers and guardians of the Ardeo core.
- Intelligent Summanir, researchers and guardians of magic and Arcana.

Their lifespan was far greater, compared to any other creature, but not eternal.
Once dead, while the body remained in the Material plane, souls, accompanied by Neiri, traveled to the Soul plane. There, they would be laid for eternal rest.
