After the defeat and closing of the Abyss gates, Lea, Malachai and other gods of the First Pantheon decided that the only logical decision was for Inis and her brethren, Avelia, Zachar and Jordeg, to be banished from the Celestial plane.
They have started with the reconstruction of the Astral gates and reestablishment of their connection with elements. As four gods had departed the First Pantheon, the purpose of it was gone, Lea had proclaimed the equality among all the gods and their realms inside the Celestial plane. She remained as the Matron and the Protector of Celestial plane. The First Pantheon was no more.
Inis, Avelia, Zachar and Jordeg have all been replaced by the younger children of the other gods.
