As the invasion started, Lea, taken aback by the extent of Inis's schemes, rushed to the Astral gate, desperate to summon the elemental forces. Though she succeeded in dispatching her message, she was met with the grim reality that the gate had already been sealed. Undeterred, Lea swiftly rallied the deities still allied with her cause, planning a defensive strategy to safeguard the elven territories and realms in the Celestial plane.

For the gods, direct intervention in the mortal realm was impossible. The elements, at the dawn of creation, had expressly forbidden them from traversing through the celestial gates. Any deity daring to defy this edict would be stripped of their immortality and rendered mortal.

Elements were late to reach Ardeo on time. Nevertheless, they managed to convey a crucial message to Malachai, the god of arcana. Deep beneath the North Stjarna ocean, concealed by the raging waters, lay a modest temple existing in both Celestial and Material plane — a creation of the elements themselves. This Astral temple holds the sole portal to the astral realm, within which are hidden chambers holding a secret. Unbeknownst to Inis, who had sealed the Astral gate located in the primary levels of the temple, there existed deeper layers where an ancient scroll was encased.

This scroll, held the power to awaken the guardians of the planet—mighty dragons and their keepers, entombed in eternal sleep. These formidable protectors, established by the elements long before the creation of the First Pantheon of gods, were the last line of defense against the encroaching darkness.
